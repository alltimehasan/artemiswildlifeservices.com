About Artemis

Greek mythology & the origin of our name

The word Artemis comes from Greek mythology. Artemis was the ancient Greek goddess of wild animals, wilderness, nature, and the hunt. Artemis was the daughter of Zeus and Leto and the twin sister of Apollo. Her special powers included perfect aim with the bow and arrow, the ability to turn herself and others into animals, healing, and control of nature.

Artemis and her passion for animals and nature inspired us and our company
